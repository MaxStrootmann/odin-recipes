Chicken Alfredo Pasta Recipe

Description

Chicken Alfredo Pasta is a creamy and luxurious pasta dish made from fettuccine noodles tossed in a rich and velvety Alfredo sauce. The tender pieces of chicken add a delightful protein punch, making it a favorite comfort dish for many.

Ingredients:

  • Fettuccine noodles
  • Boneless chicken breasts, sliced
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Boil the fettuccine noodles according to package instructions until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced chicken breasts and cook until golden brown and fully cooked. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, melt butter and add minced garlic. Saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add heavy cream to the skillet and bring to a gentle simmer. Reduce the heat to low.
  5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Add the cooked fettuccine noodles to the skillet and toss to coat with the Alfredo sauce.
  7. Add the cooked chicken slices back to the skillet and stir gently to combine.
  8. Once everything is well-combined and heated through, remove from heat.
  9. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and additional grated Parmesan if desired.
  10. Serve warm and enjoy!
