Spicy Shrimp Tacos Recipe

Description

Spicy Shrimp Tacos are a delightful combination of flavorful shrimp, tangy slaw, and a spicy cream sauce, all nestled inside a soft tortilla. It's a quick and vibrant dish that's perfect for a refreshing summer meal.

Ingredients:

  • Large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Soft taco tortillas
  • Red cabbage, thinly sliced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime, juiced
  • Chili powder
  • Ground cumin
  • Garlic powder
  • Olive oil
  • Sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • Sriracha or hot sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional toppings: sliced avocado, diced red onion, jalape√±o slices

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix chili powder, ground cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Add shrimp to the bowl and toss until well coated with the spice mix.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned shrimp and cook until they turn pink on both sides, around 1-2 minutes per side. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine thinly sliced red cabbage, chopped cilantro, and lime juice. Season with salt and toss to mix. This will be your tangy slaw.
  4. In another bowl, mix together sour cream (or Greek yogurt) with Sriracha or hot sauce to make a spicy cream sauce. Adjust spiciness according to your preference.
  5. Warm the taco tortillas according to package instructions.
  6. To assemble the tacos, place a generous amount of slaw on each tortilla, top with a few shrimps, drizzle with the spicy cream sauce, and add any additional toppings you prefer.
  7. Serve immediately with lime wedges on the side and enjoy!
